# Day 3 — Constitution — Article 15 Revision Micro-Drill

## Study Label

Week 1 — Day 3 — Wednesday — 13.05.2026

## Topic

Article 15 — quick revision before Article 16.

## Learner Answers and Feedback

### Q1. Article 15 applies to citizen or person?

Learner answer:

> Article 15 applies to Citizen of India.

Feedback:

Correct. Article 15 applies to citizens, unlike Article 14 which applies to any person.

Refined answer:

> Article 15 applies to citizens of India.

---

### Q2. What does “grounds only of” mean?

Learner answer:

> "Grounds only of" in Article 15 specifies the grounds on which any discrimination is prohibited. Which are Religion, Race, Case, Sex, Place of Birth or any of them.

Feedback:

Correct in substance. Typographical correction: “Case” should be “caste”. The phrase “grounds only of” means discrimination solely on one or more of the listed grounds.

Refined answer:

> “Grounds only of” means discrimination solely on the basis of religion, race, caste, sex, place of birth, or any of them.

---

### Q3. Article 15(3) protects whom?

Learner answer:

> Article 15(3) protects Women and Children.

Feedback:

Correct. More precise wording: Article 15(3) enables the State to make special provisions for women and children.

Refined answer:

> Article 15(3) allows the State to make special provisions for women and children.

---

### Q4. Article 15(6) relates to which category?

Learner answer:

> Article 15(6) relates to Economically Weaker Section (EWS)

Feedback:

Correct. Article 15(6) relates to economically weaker sections of citizens other than the classes covered by Articles 15(4) and 15(5).

Refined answer:

> Article 15(6) relates to economically weaker sections of citizens, excluding the classes covered by Articles 15(4) and 15(5).

---

### Q5. What is the exception under Article 15(5)?

Learner answer:

> That there should be reservation for SEBC, SC/ST, and Educationally Backward class in all Educational Institutions, private or public, except any minority led institution.

Feedback:

Correct direction. Refine the wording: Article 15(5) permits special provisions by law for advancement of SEBC, SC, and ST in relation to admission to educational institutions, including private aided or unaided institutions. The exception is minority educational institutions under Article 30(1).

Refined answer:

> Article 15(5) permits special provisions for SEBC, SC, and ST in admissions to educational institutions, including private aided or unaided institutions, but excludes minority educational institutions under Article 30(1).

## Overall Assessment

Article 15 revision status: good.

## Refinement Points

1. Article 15 applies to citizens; Article 14 applies to any person.
2. “Grounds only of” means discrimination solely on the listed grounds.
3. Correct ground: caste, not “case”.
4. Article 15(5) exception: minority educational institutions under Article 30(1).
5. Article 15(6) EWS excludes classes covered by Articles 15(4) and 15(5).
