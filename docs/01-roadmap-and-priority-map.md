## Article / Section Focus

### Constitution

**Must read:** Articles 12, 13, 14, 19, 21, 32, 136, 141, 142, 226, 300A, 368.

**Read after core:** Articles 15, 16, 17, 18, 20, 22, 23, 24, 25–30, DPSP, emergency provisions, judiciary, basic structure.

### CPC

**Must read:** Sections 2, 9, 10, 11, 20, 80, 96, 100, 104, 115; Orders 1, 2, 6, 7, 8, 9, 11, 17, 21, 39, 41, 43.

### Evidence / BSA

**Must read:** Relevancy, admissions, confessions, dying declarations, burden of proof, presumptions, documentary evidence, witnesses, examination.

### BNS / BNSS

**BNS:** definitions, general exceptions, abetment, attempt, hurt, culpable homicide, murder, theft, robbery, cheating, criminal breach of trust.

**BNSS:** FIR, investigation, arrest, bail, charge, trial, judgment, appeal, revision, limitation, compounding.

### Contract

**Must read:** proposal, acceptance, consideration, free consent, void agreements, contingent contracts, indemnity, guarantee, bailment, agency.
